About

Reducing friction between people and systems.

I'm a Transformation & Change PM currently completing a Specialized Master's in Innovation & Transformation Management at CentraleSupélec (top 3 engineering school in France), after a Global BBA at ESSEC Business School and an exchange year at UC Berkeley focused on innovation and change management.

Over nearly two years as a Transformation Consultant in the Innovation & Technology Division at Crédit Agricole CIB, I drove business and IT transformation initiatives — redesigning processes, leading market research across fintech and deep tech, and translating complex requirements into clear scopes and value propositions for senior stakeholders.

I work at the intersection where operational reality meets technical capability: I understand how business teams think under pressure, and I can hold a technical conversation without needing things simplified. That combination is what lets me cut through the friction that slows transformation down.

Bilingual French/English, conversational in Spanish, and genuinely excited about AI-driven transformation — I use tools like Claude, Gemini, and Copilot as part of how I work, not just topics I present on.
